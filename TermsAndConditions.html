8. Grievance Redressal Mechanism

Step 1: Direct Contact

In case of any grievance/complaint, an investor may approach the concerned Research Analyst who shall strive to redress the grievance immediately, but not later than 21 days of receipt.

Step 2: SMARTODR Platform

If not satisfied with the resolution, investors can file complaints on SMARTODR platform for resolution through online conciliation or arbitration.

Step 3: SEBI SCORES

Clients can lodge grievances through SEBI'S SCORES platform at www.scores.sebi.gov.in

Review Process: Complaints on SCORES 2.0 undergo a two-level review:

  1. First review done by designated body (RAASB)
  2. Second review done by SEBI
